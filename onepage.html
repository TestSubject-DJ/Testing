Gino Colombi is a dynamic DJ and producer hailing from Rosario, Argentina. With over a decade dedicated to mastering the art of mixing, Gino has seamlessly transitioned into music production, delivering tracks that resonate across the House music spectrum. His versatility shines through in his ability to blend Old School House vibes with Tech House and Techno elements, creating a unique sound that captivates audiences worldwide.

As the founder of "House Is Life" Records, Gino is committed to pushing the boundaries of electronic music, fostering a community that appreciates the depth and soul of House music.
