Artist Bio

Gino Colombi is an underground house DJ and producer from Rosario, Argentina, blending Old School House, Tech House, UK Garage, and Techno with a fresh, modern edge. As founder of House Is Life Records, he champions vinyl culture while pushing the sound forward with trancey synths, chopped vocals, and gritty analog textures.

With over a decade behind the decks and releases charting on Beatport and Traxsource, Gino crafts high-energy sets that bridge nostalgia and innovation. His tracks are supported by respected underground labels worldwide, bringing raw house grooves from Buenos Aires to global dance floors.
